Welcome to Connect-4!


Rules:

1. Decide who will be player 1 and 2. Only one player plays at a time.
2. On your turn, click one of the spots you want to place your disk in.
3. An error will pop up if you try to put a disk in a spot it cant go in
4. The game ends when there is 4-in-a-row (horizontally, vertically, or
diagonally), or a stalemate
5. The winner goes first on the next game
